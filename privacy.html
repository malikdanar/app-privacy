Hidden Tiles Saga

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Hidden Tiles Saga handles information when you use the app. The app itself is designed as a casual puzzle experience and does not ask you to create an account or submit personal details.

No registration
No in-app analytics by us
No advertising SDKs by us

In short, the app does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information about you on our own servers.

2. What we do not collect

The Hidden Tiles Saga app does not collect or process the following types of data for our own use:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers
  • Location data (GPS or precise location)
  • Payment or billing information
  • Contact lists, calendars, or photos
  • Health, fitness, or other sensitive data

We do not operate any backend servers for user tracking or profiling related to this app.

3. Game board and third-party content

Hidden Tiles Saga displays a puzzle experience that is rendered from an external game board hosted on the internet. This means that:

  • The app opens and displays interactive content provided by a third-party site.
  • That third-party site may process technical information (for example, IP address, device type, or basic usage data) in accordance with its own privacy policy.
  • We do not control, operate, or directly access any servers belonging to that third-party.

We strongly recommend that you review the privacy policy of the game provider whose content is shown in the app, as their practices may differ from ours.

4. Information stored on your device

The app may store small pieces of information locally on your device in order to:

  • Remember the last opened board or screen inside the game board
  • Improve loading behavior and reduce waiting times
  • Preserve certain technical markers from the embedded board

This information can include:

  • A saved link that allows the app to reopen the same board later
  • Technical markers related to the embedded board’s browsing session

These items are stored only on your device (for example, using the system’s preferences storage). They are not transmitted by us to our own servers.

5. Children’s privacy

Hidden Tiles Saga is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not require registration or submission of personal details, we do not have a way to identify individual users, including children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information to a third-party game provider shown inside the app, please review that provider’s privacy policy and contact them directly if necessary.

6. Data sharing and transfers

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

Any technical information processed by the third-party game board is handled by that provider under its own terms and policies. We do not receive detailed user profiles or contact information from that provider.

7. Security

Hidden Tiles Saga does not maintain its own user database or cloud service which stores personal information. Local information saved on your device is handled by the operating system’s standard mechanisms.

Please keep your device and operating system up to date and use a secure lock method to protect local data stored on your phone or tablet.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements to the app or changes in legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

Continued use of Hidden Tiles Saga after an update means that you accept the revised Privacy Policy.
